💡 Travel Tips
🎫
Smart Card = 10% DiscountSmart card gives 10% off every trip. Min balance ₹50 required to travel.
⏰
Avoid Peak HoursRush hours 8–10 AM & 5–8 PM. Travel earlier/later for comfort & speed.
🚺
Ladies CoachesFirst & last coach reserved for ladies. Men strictly avoid these during all times.
♿
Wheelchair AccessAll stations have lifts and ramps. Priority seating available in every coach.
🎒
Security CheckAll bags through scanner. Carry valid ID. Arrive 5 min early during rush hours.
📶
Free WiFi at StationsDMRC offers free WiFi at major stations. Free WiFi available at major stations — check with station staff for network name.
💧
Carry Water in SummerDelhi summer is extreme. Carry water bottle. Some stations have water coolers.
🔋
Keep Phone ChargedCarry a power bank. Charging points available at select stations only.
🚫 Banned Items
⚠️ Not Allowed in Delhi Metro
Carrying these items is a punishable offense under Metro Railway Act 2002.
💣
Explosives & FirearmsAll weapons, firearms, ammunition, and explosives strictly banned. Fine + arrest.
🔪
Sharp WeaponsKnives >6cm, scissors, swords not allowed. Small nail cutters permitted.
🧪
Flammable MaterialsPetrol, kerosene, paint, thinner, gas cylinders strictly prohibited.
🚬
SmokingStrictly prohibited inside metro & premises. Fine: ₹200.
🍺
AlcoholAlcohol consumption or carrying open containers banned. Fine: ₹200.
🐾
AnimalsPets not allowed except certified guide dogs with valid certification.
📦
Oversized LuggageMax allowed: 60×45×25 cm or 15 kg. Oversized items not permitted in coach.

💰 Smart Card Recharge
💳 Recharge Your DMRC Smart Card
Online recharge via official DMRC portal, any UPI app, or at any station Token Vending Machine (TVM).
1️⃣
At TVM MachineVisit Token Vending Machine at any metro station. Accepts cash + cards.
2️⃣
DMRC Official AppVisit the official DMRC website or use any UPI app to recharge your smart card online.
3️⃣
WhatsApp RechargeVisit official DMRC portal or app for WhatsApp recharge service.
4️⃣
Minimum BalanceKeep min ₹50 on card. Card with zero balance cannot be used for travel.
📱 Book on WhatsApp
📱 QR Code Ticket Booking
Book QR code metro tickets instantly via official DMRC digital channels.
1️⃣
Save DMRC NumberVisit official DMRC website for the current WhatsApp booking number (check dmrc.com for latest contact)
2️⃣
Send "Hi"Send "Hi" or "Ticket" to start the DMRC chatbot booking process.
3️⃣
Choose RouteSelect source and destination station from the options sent by chatbot.
4️⃣
Pay via UPIPay using UPI, debit/credit card, or net banking. Amount deducted instantly.
5️⃣
Get QR CodeQR ticket received on WhatsApp. Scan at metro gate AFC. Valid for that day.
